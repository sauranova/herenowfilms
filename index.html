Who We Are

Our Mission

HereNow Films Pvt Ltd is a Gujarat-based creative company founded by acclaimed Gujarati actor Mayur Soneji and Mitali Mayur Soneji. We run a film school, produce cinematic brand content, manage events, and create advertising — all under one roof.

Our signature production, Vasundhara — Zameen Ke Super Heroes, set a new benchmark for brand storytelling in India. Through our film school, we nurture the next generation of Gujarati filmmakers.
