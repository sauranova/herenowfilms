Who We Are

HereNow Films

HereNow Films Pvt Ltd is a Gujarat-based creative company co-founded in 2018 by Mayur Soneji — a celebrated Gujarati film actor — and his partner Mitali Mayur Soneji. What began as a passion for cinema education has grown into a full-service creative house spanning a film school, production company, advertising studio, and event management agency.

Mayur's experience on sets of acclaimed Gujarati films — from the historical epic Kasoombo to the action drama Victor 303 — shapes every lesson taught at HereNow. Students learn not from textbooks but from a working actor who has lived the craft.

Our signature production, Vasundhara — Zameen Ke Super Heroes, demonstrates that HereNow can deliver cinema-quality storytelling for brands. We believe every business, every student, and every story deserves to be told with the same care a filmmaker brings to the screen.

How We Work

Our Process

Four deliberate stages — each one a discipline in its own right.

01

Ideation & Script

Every production at HereNow starts with the story. Whether a brand campaign or a feature film, we develop the concept from ground up with creative integrity.

02

Pre-Production

From casting calls and location scouts to shot lists and storyboards — our film school background means every detail is planned with a director's eye.

03

Production

On set, students learn alongside professionals. Our productions double as live classrooms, guided by Mayur Soneji's hands-on filmmaking experience.

04

Post & Delivery

Editing, colour grading, sound design, and delivery — we handle the complete post-production pipeline to ensure every project meets cinematic standards.
